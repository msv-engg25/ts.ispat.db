Our Journey

Three decades of innovation and engineering excellence

Founded in 1986 by the visionary industrialist Late Tarsem Singh, T.S. ISPAT Private Limited began as a small workshop dedicated to providing quality engineering solutions for the steel industry. With unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, the company quickly established itself as a reliable partner for rolling mill equipment.

Over the years, under the leadership of Narinder Pal Singh, the company expanded its operations, incorporating cutting-edge technology and modern manufacturing practices. Today, T.S. ISPAT stands as a testament to Indian engineering excellence, serving clients across the globe with state-of-the-art rolling mill machinery.

With Director Jasdeep Kaur leading innovation initiatives, T.S. ISPAT continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in rolling mill technology, focusing on sustainability, efficiency, and precision engineering.

Leadership Team

The visionaries guiding T S ISPAT towards excellence

Late Tarsem Singh

Founder (1945-2015)

A visionary entrepreneur and engineer, Tarsem Singh laid the foundation of what would become one of India's premier rolling mill machinery manufacturers. With his innovative approach and unwavering commitment to quality, he established T.S. ISPAT in 1986.

His engineering acumen, combined with a profound understanding of customer needs, enabled the company to develop machinery that set new standards in the industry. Under his guidance, T.S. ISPAT grew from a small workshop to a recognized name in the steel processing industry.

Though no longer with us, his principles of excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction continue to guide the company's operations and philosophy.

Narinder Pal Singh

Chairman

Narinder Pal Singh, the son of our founder, has been at the helm of T.S. ISPAT since 2010. With an engineering background, he brings a perfect blend of technical expertise and business acumen to the company.

Under his leadership, T.S. ISPAT has expanded its global footprint, entering markets across Asia, Europe, and Africa. His strategic vision has led to the modernization of manufacturing facilities and the implementation of cutting-edge technologies.

Narinder is particularly passionate about sustainable manufacturing practices and has led initiatives to make T.S. ISPAT's operations more environmentally responsible and energy-efficient.

Jasdeep Kaur

Director

As Director of Operations and Innovation, Jasdeep Kaur has been instrumental in transforming T.S. ISPAT's manufacturing capabilities and product design. With a background in economics and extensive experience in the steel industry, she leads the company's R&D initiatives.

Jasdeep has spearheaded the development of T.S. ISPAT's latest generation of rolling mill equipment, incorporating smart manufacturing principles and IoT capabilities. Her focus on precision engineering and quality control has further enhanced the company's reputation for reliability.

In addition to her technical contributions, Jasdeep has been a strong advocate for diversity in manufacturing and has established training programs to develop the next generation of engineering talent.
